OUR EXPERTISE

What We Do?

BRAKE REPAIR

If your brakes squeal, vibrate, or feel soft, its time for an inspection. Our experts provide full brake service — including pad replacement, rotor resurfacing, and fluid checks — to ensure your safety on the road.

ENGINE REPAIR

When the check engine light comes on, it can mean several possible issues — from a faulty oxygen sensor or catalytic converter to a mass airflow problem. Our skilled technicians use advanced diagnostic tools to identify the exact cause and perform the necessary repairs to restore your engine’s performance and reliability.

TIRE REPAIR & REPLACEMENT

Whether you got a puncture or worn-out treads, we will get you rolling again. We offer tire patching, rotation, balancing, and replacement. Not sure which tire fits your car best? Our team will guide you on all-season, performance, and off-road options.

COOLING SYSTEM SERVICE

Prevent engine overheating and costly damage. We inspect, flush, and refill your radiator and coolant system to keep your engine running at optimal temperature — especially in Malaysias hot weather.

BATTERY SERVICE & REPLACEMENT

When your car wont start or makes that clicking sound, it might be a weak or dead battery. We perform quick battery health checks, charging system tests, and replacements using trusted brands.
